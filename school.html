About our school

"Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can be achieved it can be achieved"

Education is an on-going process which encourages and enthuses us to explore into the galaxy of dream and vision. Jeevan Jyoti International School is a progressive, Co-educational, English Medium Institution situated in a pollution-free, eco-friendly environment, sprawling over two acres of land with state-of-art infrastructure for perfect grooming of the children.

Principal message

Today Jeevan Jyoti International School stands as a pinnacle among the institutions of Hodal. This has been possible due to persistent hard work and sincere efforts of JJIS Fraternity. As the Principal of the school, my heart swells with pride when I see my students going back home with laurels. The students of Jeevan Jyoti International School have added yet another feather in the crown of the school with their marvelous performance in Board Result (2023-24). I congratulate all the parents and students and thank them for believing in us.

Chairman message

Jeevan Jyoti International School, came into existence by a noted Mr. Birpal Singh, who firmly believes that Indian students who are the future of the country have much more potential than what is being seen by the world. Keeping this in mind, he thought of contributing to the cause and getting students groomed in a different way, recognizing talent in students and enhancing it in a way that every student is able to convert his dreams to realities.
